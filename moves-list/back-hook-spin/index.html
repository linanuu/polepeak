Beginner Static

Back Hook Spin

Back Hook Spin is a beginner static move, great for building hip and leg awareness against the pole before moving into dynamic spins.

Pole Peak Tutorial
Top tips
  • Keep your standing leg strong and grounded for balance
  • Hook your back knee behind the pole, not your calf
  • Engage your core to stay lifted rather than sinking into your hip
