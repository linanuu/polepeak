Archer

Learn the Archer — An advanced dreamy move possible from different entries - pre-req to Archer split. Entries possible from a variety of moves such as Hangglider or Outside leg hang.

Pole Peak Tutorial
Top tips
  • Build a secure invert.
  • Feel secure in Outside leg hang and/or Hangglider depending on entry choice
  • Ensure you have good grip in hands and shoulder area.
  • This is not so much a strength move as it is ensuring you have the correct contact points.
  • Learn it over a crash mat and build the arch gradually, ideally with a spotter until the position feels secure.
